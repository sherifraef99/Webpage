Countries I Want To Visit

  • Japan

    Japan is a fantastic, diverse destination with lots of things to eat, see, and do. At first sight, Japan may appear to be overpowering. Consider the absence of all street signs. A plethora of sparkling lights. Toilet-seats from the future. The dreaded capsule hotel rooms. While some could call this a sensory overload, others might see it as an exciting challenge.

  • Malaysia

    Malaysia's variety is what makes it so intriguing. In the same nation, you may see bustling modern cities, colonial heritage, relax on some of the world's most stunning beaches, and climb through lush green forests.

  • Norway

    There are several reasons to visit Norway. Norway has mountains, fjords, the Northern Lights, Sami culture, whale watching, and much more to offer, and the people are ready to welcome you. You will enjoy once-in-a-lifetime experiences and the possibility to tick something off your bucket list if you visit Norway.
